# Sample Evaluation Questions

## Factual

- What is Coulomb's law?
- What is electric flux?

## Conceptual

- Why do electric field lines not intersect?
- What is the physical meaning of electric potential?

## Formula-Based

- What is the expression for the electric field due to a point charge?
- State Gauss's law in mathematical form.

## Comparison

- Compare conductors and insulators.
- Compare electric field and electric potential.

## Multi-Section

- How does Gauss's law help derive the electric field due to a uniformly charged sphere?

## Unsupported

- What does the document say about quantum computing applications?
